Who's Coming to the Zoo?

The North Carolina Zoo and Botanical Gardens in Asheboro, North Carolina is currently developing a new 12-acre Asia Continent exhibit scheduled to open in 2026. The new exhibit is planned to feature tigers, Komodo dragons, white-cheeked gibbons and many other iconic species from across Asia.
